Common questions

Is Parrot a replacement for speech therapy?

No. Parrot is a practice tool meant to complement homework assigned by a speech-language pathologist, not a substitute for professional therapy or diagnosis.

Does Parrot sync across devices?

Not yet. Parrot currently stores word banks and session history on-device only.

Can I add my own words?

Yes — you can add any target word and tag it with the sound and position your therapist assigned.

I found a bug. What should I include in my report?

Your iOS version, iPhone model, and the steps that led to the issue. Screenshots help a lot too.
